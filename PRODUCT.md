# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Business founders, CTOs, and operations leads at SMEs and scale-ups who know AI is strategic but lack the in-house expertise to execute it. They arrive with budget authority, vague AI goals, and skepticism about vendors who overpromise. They need a trusted technical partner who speaks both strategy and engineering.

## Product Purpose

Anaai.tech is the public face of Oluwatosin Ayeni's AI consulting practice. It exists to convert qualified leads into discovery calls by demonstrating deep technical credibility and a clear, results-oriented process. Success = a booked Calendly call with a serious prospect.

## Brand Personality

Expert precision. Quiet confidence. Execution-first.

The brand says "I've shipped this before" not "here's what AI can do for you." Peer-to-peer, not vendor-to-client. The tone is senior-engineer-turned-strategist: calm, direct, no hype.

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS "AI platform" marketing (glowing orbs, teal gradients, "10x your productivity")
- Consultant-bro personal brands (loud testimonials, urgency countdowns, "LIMITED SPOTS")
- Corporate consulting firms (McKinsey-sterile, stock-photo heavy, committee-written copy)

## Design Principles

1. **Execution speaks** — every design choice should communicate "this person has shipped real systems." Precision over decoration.
2. **Confidence without noise** — strong hierarchy, restrained palette. Let the work and credentials carry the weight, not visual spectacle.
3. **Friction-free conviction** — the path from landing to booking a call should feel inevitable, not pushy. Remove every hesitation point.
4. **Cinematic restraint** — the dark cinematic aesthetic earns credibility in the AI space. Keep it intentional: light used to direct attention, not to impress.
5. **Copy is the product** — every headline, label, and CTA is a sales argument. Specificity beats cleverness.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA as a minimum. Respect prefers-reduced-motion — animations are cinematic touches, not requirements. All interactive elements keyboard-navigable.
